What is VISIONALTRIX?

It is an AI gateway that learns from rare, high-impact racing crashes and converts them into synthetic, explainable test cases for real-world automotive systems. Our goal is to close the gap between elite motorsport safety intelligence and everyday passenger vehicles.

1. F1 Telemetry Ingestion

Normalize, denoise, and align racing data (velocity, yaw, G-force, suspension) from multiple sources.

2. Anomaly Detection

We detect outlier crash signatures that normal datasets barely have — rollovers, split-second side impacts.

3. Safety Scenario Generator

Outliers are translated into road-level events that ADAS, lane-keep, or collision avoidance can train on.

4. Reporting & Explainability

We log “why” a crash is flagged, ideal for regulators, OEM R&D teams, and hackathon demo judges 😉

Why F1 data first?

Race incidents create edge-case dynamics — multi-axis impacts, abrupt deceleration, aero stall, wheel-to-wheel contacts — that road datasets almost never capture. Training on these gives road AIs a “stress-tested” baseline.

FAQ for judges / examiners

Why not train directly on road data?

Road data is abundant but lacks extreme crash patterns. F1 gives us those “rare but important” signatures.

How do you ensure privacy/compliance?

We strip identifiers, store only signals, and log only inference metadata.

Can it run in real-time?

Yes, our pipeline targets sub-50ms inference on moderate hardware.

What is the output?

JSON with anomaly score, scenario label, and safety actions.
